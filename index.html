Who I Am

About Me

I am a researcher specialising in hydrology and remote sensing, with a PhD focused on advancing our understanding of water systems through innovative satellite technologies, specifically the SWOT mission and computational methods.

Research Focus

My work bridges the gap between satellite remote sensing and practical water resource management. I develop methodologies to monitor river systems, predict floods, and understand hydrological processes at multiple scales using cutting-edge remote sensing data and hydrological model.
