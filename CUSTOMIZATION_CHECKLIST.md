## Quick Find & Replace

Use your text editor's find & replace function:

- Find: `[Your Name]` → Replace with: `Dr. John Doe`
- Find: `[University Name]` → Replace with: `Massachusetts Institute of Technology`
- Find: `[Your Field]` → Replace with: `Hydrology`
- Find: `your.email@university.edu` → Replace with: `jdoe@mit.edu`
- Find: `[City, Country]` → Replace with: `Cambridge, MA, USA`
- Find: `[Year]` → Replace with: `2024`, `2020`, etc.
